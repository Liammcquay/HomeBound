About Us

We are a couple of friends who see the trouble and inconvenience of people living in these circumstances. With this happening in our society, we want to make a difference and help others get back up on their feet and become self-sufficient again.

We constantly see people sitting along the streets and bridges, which we occasionally donate money to when we have cash on us.

We hope we can improve their lives with our project.
